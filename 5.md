41. What are aggregate functions?

Aggregate functions perform calculations on data.

Examples:

COUNT

SUM

AVG

MAX

MIN

42. What does COUNT do?

COUNT returns the number of rows.

43. What does SUM do?

SUM calculates total value.

44. What does AVG do?

AVG calculates the average value.

45. What does MAX do?

MAX returns the largest value.

46. What does MIN do?

MIN returns the smallest value.

47. What is UPPER function?

UPPER converts text to uppercase.

48. What is LOWER function?

LOWER converts text to lowercase.

49. What is LENGTH function?

LENGTH returns the length of a string.

50. What is ROUND function?

ROUND rounds numeric values.
